Services

Technical Services

Readymix (W.I.) Limited is the only pre-mixed concrete supplier in Trinidad and Tobago that offers a well-equipped laboratory, headed by a qualified Civil Engineer.

Our team of Laboratory Technicians is also qualified and trained to observe strict quality practices, in accordance with ASTM, British and ISO Standards. Readymix (W.I.) Limited has attained ISO 9001:2000 certification - demonstration of our continued commitment to research, development and innovation in concrete technology.

The Technical team extends its competencies to our clients and offers the following concrete and aggregate tests:-

  • Slumps
  • Cube
  • Pundit
  • Schmidt Hammer
  • Cores
  • Organize and silt analysis

The technical team can also design appropriate mixes to suit your needs. We are also able to consult with our clients on complex concrete needs such as underwater applications, air entrainment, low temperature and sulphate resisting concrete, as well as technical training.

Consultancy

Readymix (W.I.) Limited offers a wide range of consultancy services, to meet your concrete and construction needs. The consultancy includes:

  • Concrete Technology
  • Specialized Concrete Technology

Client Services

Unlike many concrete suppliers in Trinidad and Tobago, Readymix (W.I.) Limited offers a credit facility to our corporate clients who meet the criteria for obtaining credit.

Clients can also access information and register queries online, and request estimates from our team

Marketing and Sales Expertise

The Trinidad and Tobago concrete market is serviced by a team of Readymix (W.I.) Limited Sales Representatives, who have a combined experience level of over 60 years in construction. Each representative that services their designated area is equipped to provide free estimates and offer sound advice.

The Sales team is supported by the Central Despatch Unit, which manages the distribution network in the organization. A comprehensive computerized system linked to the concrete plant operations monitors all outgoing concrete and ensures that each customer receives on-time delivery. Readymix (W.I.) Limited is the only pre-mixed concrete supplier in Trinidad and Tobago to provide a computerized delivery service.
